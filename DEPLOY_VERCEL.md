## Deploy to Vercel (2 minutes):

1. **Go to Vercel**: https://vercel.com
2. **Sign in** with your GitHub account
3. **Import Project**:
   - Click "Add New..." button (top right)
   - Click "Project"
   - Find and select your `valyxis` repository
   - Click "Import"
4. **Configure** (usually auto-detected):
   - Framework Preset: `Other` (or leave default)
   - Root Directory: `./` (leave as is)
   - Build Command: Leave **empty** (no build needed)
   - Output Directory: Leave **empty** (serves from root)
   - Install Command: Leave **empty**
5. **Deploy**:
   - Click "Deploy" button
   - Wait 1-2 minutes
   - âœ… Your site is live!

## Your Live Website:

After deployment, Vercel will give you a URL like:
- `https://valyxis.vercel.app`
- Or `https://valyxis-steponaskaciulis-max.vercel.app`

You can also set up a custom domain in Vercel settings!
